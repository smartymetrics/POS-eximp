# Legal Dashboard: Using the "Mark Closed" Button

## Overview

The **"Mark Closed"** button is a legal status marker that signals final Contract of Sale (COS) delivery and completion within the legal workflow. This button allows lawyers to indicate that a contract has been fully executed and delivered to the client.

**Important:** Marking a contract as closed is a **legal team action only** and does **NOT** affect the sales pipeline stage or any sales/invoice data. It is purely for legal tracking and closure.

---

## When to Use "Mark Closed"

You should click **"Mark Closed"** when:

✅ The contract has been **fully signed** (all 3 parties have signed):
   - Witness 1 (External witness)
   - Client/Purchaser
   - Legal Authority (sealed)

✅ The **final executed PDF** has been generated and delivered to the client

✅ The contract execution is complete from a **legal perspective**

---

## How to Mark a Contract as Closed

### Step 1: Open the Legal Dashboard

1. Navigate to the **Legal Dashboard** from your portal menu
2. You will see a table of all contracts with their current statuses

### Step 2: Locate the Contract

- Search by **Invoice Number**, **Client Name**, or **Email** using the search box at the top
- Find the contract in the table that is ready to be closed

### Step 3: Check Contract Status

Look for contracts showing:
- **Status Badge:** "COMPLETED" or "SEALED"
- **Signatures:** "3 / 3 Signatures" (all parties have signed)
- **Action Button:** "Mark Closed" (emerald green button)

### Step 4: Click "Mark Closed"

1. Click the **"Mark Closed"** button in the **Actions** column
2. A confirmation dialog will appear:

   ```
   "Mark this Contract of Sale as legally closed for the lawyer team? 
    This will not change the sales pipeline stage."
   ```

3. Review the message and click **"Yes"** to confirm
4. The button will show a loading state briefly
5. Once complete, you will see a success message: **"Contract marked closed"**

### Step 5: Verify the Status

After marking as closed:
- The contract row will update to show **"CLOSED"** in the status column
- The action button will change to a **"Closed"** badge (emerald green)
- The contract is now recorded as legally closed in the system

---

## Alternative: Mark Closed from the Orchestration Modal

You can also mark a contract as closed from the detailed **Status Modal**:

### Steps:

1. Click on any contract row to open the **Orchestration Modal**
2. Scroll to the **Actions** section at the bottom
3. Once all 3 signatures are verified (✓ indicators), the **"Mark Closed"** button will appear
4. Click **"Mark Closed"**
5. Confirm the action in the dialog
6. The modal will refresh and show the "Closed" status

---

## What Happens After Marking Closed?

**For the Legal Team:**
- The contract is recorded as legally closed in the system
- An activity log entry is created for audit purposes
- The contract appears in your **Legal Archive** for future reference

**For Sales & Admin:**
- ❌ The invoice **pipeline stage is NOT changed**
- ❌ The sales rep's commission status is **NOT affected**
- ❌ No automated email notifications are sent
- ✅ The legal closure status is recorded for compliance

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **This action is permanent.** Once a contract is marked closed, it cannot be reopened through the UI. If you mark it closed by mistake, contact your administrator.

⚠️ **Only for fully executed contracts.** Ensure all 3 parties have signed before clicking "Mark Closed."

⚠️ **Does not affect sales.** This is a legal-only action and does not impact invoice payments, pipeline stages, or sales workflows.

## Troubleshooting

### "Mark Closed" Button is Disabled or Not Showing

**Reason:** The contract is not yet ready to be closed.

**Check:**
- Is the contract status "COMPLETED"?
- Do all 3 signatures show ✓?
- Has the final executed PDF been generated?

**If all are true:** Refresh your browser and try again.

---

### "Contract already marked closed" Message

**Reason:** This contract was already marked as closed by another legal team member.

**Action:** No further action needed. You can verify the closed status in the contract row.

---

### Cannot Find the Contract

**Solution:**
1. Use the search box to filter by Invoice Number, Client Name, or Email
2. Check if the contract is still in "Active" view (not archived)
3. If still not visible, contact your administrator
