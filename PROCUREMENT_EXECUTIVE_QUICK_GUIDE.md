## 🚨 UNDERSTANDING THE RISKS

### Risk Matrix

| Risk | What It Means | What To Do |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| **High-Value Items** | Few items cost lots | Get multiple quotes |
| **Concentration** | Budget too dependent on one supplier | Diversify suppliers |
| **Equipment Rental** | Heavy rental dependency | Negotiate long-term rates |
| **Labour Costs** | Staff/daily wages high | Consider mechanization |
