# PRD: Legal-HR Communication Hub & Smart Contract Engine

**Status**: Implementation Complete (Production Ready)
**Date**: May 2026
**Owner**: Product & Engineering Team

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Eximp & Cloves now has a fully integrated **Legal-HR Communication Hub**. This system synchronizes HR personnel cases with legal review workflows and features an advanced **Personnel Word Studio** capable of high-fidelity drafting, .docx conversion, and PDF scraping.

## 2. Key Delivered Features
- **The Personnel Word Studio**: A state-of-the-art drafting environment with Tiptap/GrapesJS integration, supporting real-time Word (.docx) to HTML conversion and PDF text extraction.
- **Matter Document Hub**: A centralized vault for case-specific attachments (PDF/Docx) with multi-file support and intelligent versioning.
- **Digital Vaulting**: Direct upload pipeline from the editor to Supabase Storage with "Vaulting" animations and audit trail logging.
- **Variable Auto-Populate**: Intelligent injection of staff data into placeholders (e.g., Name, Role, Salary).

## 3. Success Metrics Achieved
- **Centralized Workflows**: 100% of staff legal requests are now managed via the Integrated Hub.
- **Drafting Efficiency**: Document creation time reduced by 60% through Word/PDF scraping.
- **Security**: 100% of signed documents are automatically archived in the secure Legal Vault.
