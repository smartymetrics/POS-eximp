HR Management System — User Manual

Eximp & Cloves Infrastructure Limited · April 2026

What is the HR Portal?

The HR Management Portal is a centralised platform for managing staff records, tracking performance, processing payroll, managing tasks, and handling disciplinary actions — all within the existing Eximp & Cloves ERP system.

The portal operates with three distinct interfaces based on your role:

HR Administration Line Manager Staff Member

Each role sees a different set of navigation items and data. This manual covers all three.

How to Access

1
Navigate to app.eximps-cloves.com/hr in your browser.
2
If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the Login page. Use your Eximp & Cloves email and password.
3
The system detects your role automatically and shows you the correct dashboard.
You can also access the HR Portal from the Legal Dashboard sidebar → "👥 HR & Personnel Portal" link at the bottom.

Goal Management

Set monthly targets for each staff member. Goals are role-specific.

Create a New Goal

1
Navigate to Goal Management.
2
Click "+ Set New Goal".
3
Select the Staff Member, enter the KPI Name (e.g., "Deals Closed"), Target Value, Unit, Period, and Weight.
4
Click "Create Goal" to save.
Goals feed directly into the 40% "Goals Achieved" component of the performance score. Make sure targets are fair and documented.

1

2
Click "Run Payroll". The system will batch-process all active staff with a base salary set in their profile.
3
The payroll table will update with gross, tax, and net amounts for each employee.
Ensure all staff profiles have their base salary set under the Finances tab before running payroll.

1

2

Request Leave

1
Go to My Presence → Click "Request Leave".
2
Select the Leave Type (Annual, Sick, Study, Maternity, Paternity, Compassionate).
3

Your request will appear as "Pending" until HR approves it. You'll see the updated status on your Presence page.

Understanding Your Performance Score

Your score is calculated from objective data wherever possible:

  • 40% Goals Achieved — How well you met your monthly KPI targets. These targets are set by HR or your line manager.
  • 20% Work Quality — Measured from KYC completion rates, ticket resolution, and other quality signals.
  • 20% Teamwork — Rated by your line manager on a 1–5 scale during review cycles.
  • 20% Initiative — Rated by your line manager on a 1–5 scale during review cycles.
If you believe your score doesn't reflect your work, speak to your line manager — they can adjust qualitative scores and add context during reviews.

Using on Mobile

Mobile Navigation

On phones and tablets (screens under 1024px), the sidebar is replaced by a slide-in drawer:

1
Tap the "Menu" button in the top-right corner of the header bar.
2
The navigation drawer slides in from the left. Tap any tab to navigate.
3
Tap outside the drawer or use the ✕ button to close it.

All stat cards, tables, and forms automatically reflow to fit your screen. Tables can be scrolled horizontally if they're wider than the screen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I can't see the HR portal, only my own dashboard?

Your role determines what you see. If you're a regular staff member, you'll only see your personal tabs. Contact HR if you believe your role should be different.

Q: My performance score seems low — how is it calculated?

60% of your score comes from objective data (goals achieved + work quality). 40% comes from your manager's review. Go to "My Performance" to see the breakdown, and speak to your manager if you need clarification.

Q: How do I reset a staff member's password?

Currently, password resets are managed from the main admin system. Contact the IT administrator or use the "Forgot Password" flow on the login page.

Q: Can I edit my own profile?

Staff members can view their profile but cannot edit it directly. All profile changes must be made by HR administrators to maintain data integrity.

Q: What happens when a staff member leaves?

HR sets the staff member's status to inactive. Their profile, performance history, and documents are retained for a minimum of 5 years for compliance purposes.
