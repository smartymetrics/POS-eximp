Lead Scoring System

Our AI-powered lead scoring evaluates 6 key factors:

1. Purchase History (0-25 pts)

Repeat buyers score higher

2. Payment Reliability (0-20 pts)

100% payers get max points

3. Engagement (0-20 pts)

Recent activity indicates interest

4. Deal Size (0-20 pts)

High-value deals weighted higher

5. Recency (0-15 pts)

Recent purchases = active

6. Open Deals (0-20 pts)

Multiple open deals high priority

Score Interpretation
80-100: 🔥 HOT (Ready to convert), 60-79: ☀️ WARM (Promising), 40-59: ❄️ COOL (Needs nurturing), 0-39: ❄️ COLD (Long-term)
