03. The Contract Lifecycle

How a deal goes from "Hello" to "Signed."

1

Pick an Invoice

Every contract is tied to a purchase. Find the client in the dashboard and click "Initiate Contract."

2

The Editor (The Workshop)

Choose a Template (like "Land Purchase Agreement"). Use {{ signatures.client_name }} to tell the system where the client should sign.

3

Execution & Link

Click "Execute." The system sends a secure, unique link to the client's email. This link is like a private key—only they can use it.

05. The Audit Certificate

Crucial

This is the "Proof."

Once a contract is signed, the system generates an Authentication Certificate. This is a PDF that proves:

  • ✔ IP Tracking: We know exactly what computer was used.
  • ✔ Timestamps: We know the exact second they signed.
  • ✔ Hash Integrity: The document is "locked"—if it's changed by even 1 letter, the certificate will show it's tampered with.

In any courtroom in Nigeria, this certificate is your shield.
