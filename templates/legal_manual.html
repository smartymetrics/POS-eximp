Executive Foreword:
The Doctrine of Digital Integrity

At Eximp & Cloves, the Legal Suite is not merely a document management tool; it is a high-fidelity execution engine designed to mitigate risk, eliminate human error, and provide an immutable audit trail for every contractual engagement.

Key Objective

Transition from fragmented paper-based logistics to a Unified Execution Framework. This ensures that every signature is captured with biometric-grade digital verification and every document is stored in a secure, encrypted vault.

I. Governance & Regulatory Compliance

Regulatory Framework

Our digital signature framework is architected to comply with the Nigerian Evidence Act regarding electronic signatures. The system captures IP addresses, timestamps, and browser metadata to ensure non-repudiation.

Identity Assurance

Each signatory is authenticated through a unique session token delivered via their registered communication channel, creating a secure link between the physical person and the digital signature.

II. Document Vetting & Due Diligence

Before a contract is "Orchestrated," it must undergo the rigorous Vetting Phase.

1

Matter Intake

Verify that the property or personnel matter exists in the CRM. Ensure all prerequisite information (Survey Plans, Bio-data) is present in the record.

2

The Vault Analysis

Access the Matter Vault to review existing deeds or titles. Use the Vault Slide-Over to check for "Superseded" versions to avoid conflicting terms.

3

Internal Vetting

Submit the draft for peer review if the matter value exceeds threshold limits. Once verified, the matter is moved to the "Orchestration" stage.

IV. Multi-Party Execution Workflow

Orchestration ensures that all required signatures are gathered in the correct sequence.

P1 Primary Signatory (The Client)

The client receives a secured, tamper-evident link. They must draw or upload their signature. IP & Device Audit is captured at the exact moment of 'Final Submission'.

P2 External Witness

Where required by law, a secondary signature is gathered from the client's witness. This signature is visually distinct and cross-referenced in the Audit Certificate.

P3 Legal Sealing (Eximp & Cloves)

The legal officer reviews the gathered signatures. If compliant, they trigger the "Verify & Seal" command. This appends the Law Firm Seal and Director's signature from the encrypted Signature Vault.

V. Digital Sealing vs. Wet Signature
Choosing the Execution Method

Option A: Digital Execution

Fully automated. The system appends high-resolution PNG signatures and seals from the vault. Best for speed and remote clients.

✔ SECURE ✔ INSTANT ✔ AUDITABLE

Option B: Wet Sealing (Hybrid)

Download the "Pre-signed DRAFT" (includes Client's signature), physically stamp it, then scan and re-upload the Fully Sealed Copy.

✔ PHYSICAL ✔ TRADITIONAL ✔ MANUALLY SCANNED

VI. Custody & Audit Integrity

Immutable Storage

Once execution is complete, the document enters "Locked Custody." It is archived in our private cloud with an accompanying Certificate of Authentication.

The Certificate of Authentication

This document is the legal "Passport" for the contract. It contains:

SHA-256 Hash Integrity Proves the document has not been altered post-sealing.
Geo-Temporal Captures Verification of physical location at time of signing.
Verification ID A unique serial number for registry cross-referencing.

Compliance Checklist

1. Naming Precision

Use Contract_Main instead of Contract_v2. Consistent names trigger Intelligent Versioning.

2. Clause Auditing

Always check if {% raw %}{{ client.fullname }}{% endraw %} tags are present in the first page preamble.

3. Vault Hygiene

Regularly flag older title drafts as "Superseded" to maintain a clean matter record.
