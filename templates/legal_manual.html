01. The Big Picture

Welcome to the Legal Suite. Our mission is to take the "boring and slow" out of legal paperwork. Instead of printing paper, mailing it, and waiting for weeks, we execute everything securely online.

The Simple Explanation: We built a system that lets clients sign securely from their phones, and allows our legal team to "stamp" the final approval in seconds. It is fast, fully digital, and completely legally binding.

03. The Contract Lifecycle

How a deal goes from a "Sale" to "Legally Binding."

1

Initiate the Contract

Find the client's invoice in the portal and click "Manage Execution." Begin the Orchestration session to start the signing process.

2

The Editor (The Document Workshop)

Review the wording. You can use special tags like {% raw %}{{ client.fullname }}{% endraw %} to automatically load the client's actual name into the contract text.

3

Import or Draft (The Workshop)

Review wording in the Personnel Word Studio. You can now import existing Word Docs (.docx) or scrape text from PDFs directly into the editor canvas.

4

Client Signs via Secure Link

The system sends a unique link to the client and external witness. They draw their signature on their phone screen. Once they finish, you get alerted that it's ready for final sealing.

04. The Personnel Word Studio

The Studio is a high-fidelity environment designed specifically for the complex drafting requirements of Eximp & Cloves legal staff.

📘 Word Doc Continuation (.docx)

Powered by mammoth.js, this tool converts Microsoft Word documents into clean, semantic HTML. It is optimized for legal structures like numbered lists and nested clauses.

Pro Tip: Ensure your Word document uses standard "Heading" styles for the best conversion results.

📕 The PDF Scraper (Text Extraction)

When editing is impossible on a finalized PDF, use Scrape. This uses pdf.js to perform a deep-scan of the document's text layer.

  • Scrape Text Only: Strips away formatting to give you the raw content for new drafts.
  • Scrape & Append: Adds the scraped text to your current cursor position in the editor.

🏷️ Variable Intelligence

The Studio "knows" who you are drafting for. You can inject live data using these tags:

05. The Document Hub & Vaulting

Every matter acts as a **Secure Vault**. Documents uploaded here are encrypted in transit and stored in our private legal-vault bucket.

Multi-File Architecture

Lawyers can maintain multiple independent documents per matter. The system manages them using Unique Filename Tracking.

Understanding File Statuses

Active

The latest available version. Ready for editing or linking.

Superseded

An older version. Kept for audit but hidden from daily view.

Deleted

Document is flagged for removal and will not be accessible.

Direct Vaulting (The Pulse)

When you click ↑ Upload, the "Vaulting Overlay" appears. This indicates that the file is being processed, versioned, and stored in the cloud. Do not close the Hub until the pulse animation completes.

06. Digital Execution

When the client and witness have signed, you must execute the contract. Digital Sealing is the fastest option.

How it works:

  1. You click "Verify & Execute".
  2. The system checks the Signature Vault for the official Eximp & Cloves seals and director signatures.
  3. It digitally "stamps" these images onto the final page of the PDF.
  4. The contract is instantly emailed to the client alongside an Audit Certificate, and immediately archived in our database.

Why use this? Because it takes 1 second and requires zero manual paper handling. It is fully authenticated by the digital audit trail.

07. Wet Signature (External)

Sometimes, a lawyer prefers to physically stamp a printed document (a "Wet Seal"), or they want to use external legal software to append their signature.

1

Download Clean PDF

The system generates the full contract (with the client's signature already on it) but leaves the legal part blank and removes the "DRAFT" watermark.

2

Upload Sealed Copy

After you have stamped it, you upload the finalized PDF back into the system.

3

Finalize & Send

Click send. The system bundles your manually sealed PDF with our digital certificate and emails it to the client.

08. The Audit Certificate

Crucial

Regardless of which Option you pick, the system ALWAYS generates an Authentication Certificate. This extra PDF proves:

  • ✔ IP Tracking: Device and location used to sign.
  • ✔ Timestamps: Exact second of submission.
  • ✔ Hash Integrity: Proves the document hasn't been tampered with.

09. Best Practices & Troubleshooting

Naming Conventions

To leverage Intelligent Versioning, keep filenames consistent. Uploading a file with the same name updates its version.

Wrong

Contract_Revised_v2.docx

Correct

Contract_Main.docx

Troubleshooting Scraping

Text is missing after a PDF Scrape?

The PDF might be an image-only scan. Use an OCR tool first, or import the Word version instead.

Formatting looks messy in the Studio?

PDF Scrapes are for text recovery, not layout. Use Edit with Word Docs for perfect layout.

Security Protocol: All links expire after 1 hour. Click "Refresh Hub" to regenerate tokens if needed.
