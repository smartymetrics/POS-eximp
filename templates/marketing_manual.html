Chapter 04

First-Touch Lead Attribution

How do we know which ad generated a sale? We use professional **First-Touch Attribution**. This credits the source that originally brought the customer into our ecosystem.

1

UTM Parameter Capture

When a user clicks a link (e.g., from a Facebook Ad), we capture utm_source, utm_medium, and utm_campaign.

2

mCID Persistence

The **Marketing Campaign ID** is locked onto the contact. Even if they don't buy for months, we remember the campaign that "hooked" them.

3

Revenue Linking

When they pay an invoice, the system automatically looks up their attribution history and credits the revenue to the original Campaign.

Chapter 11

Compliance & Laws

Critical

Sending emails to people who don't want them is illegal.

1. The Automatic Link

Every single email sent contains an unsubscribe link at the footer. You do not need to add it yourself; it is mandatory.

2. Instant & Permanent Block

The moment a contact clicks "Unsubscribe", their status is set to false. Our engine checks this status the split-second before dispatching any future email. They are silently blocked forever.

NEVER manually override an unsubscribe. It is a legal violation under GDPR and Nigerian Data Protection laws.
